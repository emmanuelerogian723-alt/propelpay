Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026 · MUTYINT / PropelPay

1. Who We Are

PropelPay is a product of MUTYINT (Multipurpose Talented Young Inventors), a technology company based in Nigeria. Contact: multipurposetalentedyounginven@gmail.com · WhatsApp: +234 704 556 0291

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data

When you register we collect your name, email, business name, phone number and currency. Required to operate your account.

Invoice & Business Data

We store invoices, proposals, client records, payment history and bank accounts you add. This data belongs to you.

Usage Data

We collect standard usage data (pages visited, features used) to improve the platform. This is anonymised and never sold.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate, store and send invoices and proposals
  • To process payments via Paystack
  • To send email and WhatsApp notifications on your behalf
  • To generate AI insights from anonymised usage patterns
  • To communicate important product and security updates

4. Third-Party Services

  • Paystack — Payment processing
  • Resend — Transactional email
  • Groq / LLaMA — AI proposal generation
  • Meta WhatsApp Business API — Message delivery

5. Data Security

Data is stored on encrypted servers. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all your data
  • Correction: Update data via Settings at any time
  • Deletion: Request full deletion by emailing us
  • Portability: Export invoices and client data as PDF/CSV

7. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. On deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Invoice records may be retained for 7 years for legal compliance.

8. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies for authentication. No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics.
